Complex Planning Capability – motionChallenge

This test measures your ability to think ahead to solve puzzles. You have 6 minutes to complete as many as you can.

  • You can drag each block up/down or left/right to create a path for the red ball.
  • Each move is counted; work carefully.
  • If you get stuck, press Skip (counts as incorrect).
  • When the red ball reaches the black hole, press Next.
